How ReWear Works

3

Swap Options

Direct Swap

  • Find an item you want and propose a swap with one of your listed items
  • The other member can accept, decline, or counter your offer
  • No points involved - perfect 1:1 exchange
  • Great for items of similar value
Browse Items to Swap

Points Redemption

  • Earn points when someone selects your items for swapping
  • Redeem points for any available item in the community
  • Flexible system for items of different values
  • No need to find a perfect 1:1 match
