How ReWear Works

Our simple process makes it easy to swap clothes and reduce fashion waste

1

Create Your Account

Sign up for free and set up your profile. This allows you to list items and start swapping.

2

List Your Items

Upload photos and details of clothing you no longer wear. Our community will see your listings.

3

Browse & Discover

Find items you love from other members. Filter by category, size, condition and more.

Swap Options

Direct Swap

Propose a 1:1 exchange with another member. They can accept, decline, or counter your offer.

  • Perfect for items of similar value
  • No points involved
  • Great way to connect with other fashion lovers

Points System

Our flexible points system makes swapping fair and easy.

  • Earn points when someone selects your items
  • Redeem points for any available item
  • No need to find a perfect 1:1 match
  • Points values based on item quality and demand

Exchange Process

1

Arrange Exchange

Once a swap is agreed, coordinate with the other member. Choose between local meetup or shipping.

2

Complete Swap

Confirm the exchange in the app. For shipped items, tracking helps ensure smooth delivery.

3

Rate & Review

Share your experience to help build our trusted community. Your feedback helps others.
